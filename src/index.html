I created this tool to demonstrate that while buying real estate is often marketed as a great investment strategy, a boring S&P 500 fund or ETF will usually outperform it over a long period. That said, a primary residence should not be seen as just an investment vehicle, but is a good way to illustrate that real estate investing is more complicated than people think.

I also added a line for a CD since it roughly grows on pace with inflation and, often, a house's value will not grow as fast as inflation.

Other factors to consider: interest on mortgage, property taxes, maintenance, insurance, rent (if investment property).
